About the Top Image

The top image shows different aspects of my brain:
  • Freesurfer parcellation of gray matter on the right hemisphere,
  • T1-weighted MRI image, plotted with MRIcron,
  • First eigen-vector of my DTI diffusion-weighted images around the corpus callosum, computed with FSL dtifit,
  • A 256-channel EEG signal of my sleep showing one slow wave followed by a spindle.
